What Tesori does

◻

Scan & identify

Point your camera at any barcode for instant product details — name, brand, description, and image pulled automatically.

◈

Rate on your terms

Score products 1–10 on a slider. Five emoji-based tiers give your ratings personality without losing precision.

▣

Capture the moment

Add a hero photo to any product — snap it in the app or choose from your library for a richer archive.

◫

Organize into collections

Group products however you think — by category, trip, season, or mood. Your archive, your structure.

◎

Follow & share

Set your profile public and share collections. Follow people whose taste you trust and discover what they love.

◑

Discover what's trending

A dedicated discovery tab surfaces the highest-rated and most-saved products across the whole Tesori community.
